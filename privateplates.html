ABOUT ME

Private Plates began in Toronto, where Chef AJ honed his craft cooking for private families, intimate dinner parties, and large celebrations. Over time, the service grew beyond one-off events into ongoing weekly meal prep for busy households — a model that proved to be both impactful and sustainable.

After relocating to Vancouver, Private Plates expanded into the Lower Mainland, now catering to families, professionals, and corporate clients across the region. While meal prep remains our specialty, we've also brought our expertise to weddings, milestone events, and in-home chef experiences, blending global flavors with local ingredients.

Chef AJ's background includes training in Michelin kitchens, collaborations with celebrity chefs around the world, cooking for diplomats, and competing on Top Chef Canada. Today, with Private Plates, he brings that same level of care and creativity into everyday dining — making it possible for clients to enjoy restaurant-quality meals, tailored to their tastes, delivered fresh each week.
